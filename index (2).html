Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

ACCUMULATION OF RESULTS: The bacterial samples have been collected from all the volunteers and the purpose of accumulating them on this web page is to provide easy access to each volunteer to see the Top 10 bacterial types found in their belly buttons. Once the specific bacteria is identified to make synthetic beef then the volunteers can check their own Top 10 bacterias whether they match with the identified bacteria. Volunteers can select their 'ID' number from drop down menu under "Test Subject Id No:". Once ID is selected, the charts will show the relevant bacterial cultures in bar and bubble charts. The volunteers can also see their "Belly Button washing frequency" on the guage chart.
